Welcome to Schlumberger Datasets

Ask an engineer how to improve an application, and they will likely say, "give me a way to see MORE DATA". Line charts are the default choice for this workflow, but they are cumbersome, especially if the data streams have different scaling and units. We have implemented Schlumberger layer heat maps, an alternate graphing method that is superior to line charts because it adds an extra dimension to the data which makes it easier to recognize patterns and points of interest.

Pressure (ESP)

Downhole pressure. Delta pressure (P1 - P2) is used to calculate the Liquid Rate (Venturi flow meter)

Pressure (IC)

Intelligent completions downhole pressure

Temperature (ESP)

Downhole temperature

Temperature (IC)

Intelligent completions temperature

VSD Frequency (ESP)

Measures the output frequency of the motor

VSD Motor Amps (ESP)

Measures the current of the motor

Choke Position Percentage (IC)

Flow control value regulates the flow or pressure of the fluid (closed = 0%)

Water Cut (IC)

The ratio of water produced compared to the volume of total liquids produced

Liquid Rate (IC)

Volume flow rate of water and oil produced

Glossary

  • Electric Submersible Pump (ESP): Equipment used in wells deeper than 12,000 feet to increase the flow of liquids when reservoirs no longer have sufficient energy to naturally produce.
  • Intelligent Completions (IC): ICs incoporate permanent downhole sensors and surface-controlled downhole flow control values, enabling you to monitor, evaluate, and actively manage production (or injection) in real time.
  • Variable Speed Driver (VSD): VSDs control motor speed so that it is not overloaded.
